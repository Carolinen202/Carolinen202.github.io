All About Me 

My name is Caroline Nelson and I am the owner of Cafe of Coffee and Cakes. My best friend and I created this treats stand in High school and opened our first location in the summer of 2018. She now runs two stores in Missouri and I have 3 opened in SC. My family and I have always shown love through baking and providing a safe place for anyone who wants to sit back and enjoy a treat!
